About Me

I am Dhanasankar K, a passionate and aspiring electronics engineer currently pursuing my Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Government College of Engineering, Erode (2021–2025). I have a strong foundation in digital systems and VLSI design, and I am actively honing my skills in Verilog, FPGA development, and embedded systems.

My academic journey began at Government Boys Higher Secondary School, Gandharvakottai, in Pudukkottai District, where I developed a deep interest in mathematics and logical reasoning. These interests naturally led me to explore the world of digital electronics, which continues to fuel my motivation for building real-time systems and innovative hardware solutions.

I am highly dedicated, quick to learn, and always excited to take on new challenges. Whether it’s building a secure digital voting machine using FSMs or implementing a real-time Sobel edge detection system on FPGA, I approach every project with passion and precision. I’m committed to growing in the semiconductor industry and contributing meaningfully through continuous learning, problem-solving, and collaboration.
